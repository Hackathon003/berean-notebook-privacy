# Privacy Policy for The Berean Notebook

**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

The Berean Notebook is a Chrome extension that helps you take notes and look up Bible verses in a side panel. This page explains what data the extension uses and how.

## What We Collect

The Berean Notebook does **not** collect, sell, or transmit any personal information about you. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

## What's Stored

All notes you type, your selected Bible translation, and your page tabs are stored **locally on your own device** using Chrome's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This data:

- Never leaves your device except as described below
- Is not accessible to us, the developer, or any third party
- Stays on your device until you clear it yourself or uninstall the extension

## What Gets Sent Externally

When you type a Bible verse reference (for example, "John 3:16"), the extension sends that reference to **[bible-api.com](https://bible-api.com)** to retrieve the verse text. This is the only external request the extension makes.

- Only the verse reference itself is sent (e.g., "John 3:16") — never your notes, your name, or any other personal information
- We do not control bible-api.com's own data practices; you can review their service directly if you have questions about how they handle requests

## No Ads, No Tracking

The Berean Notebook contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking scripts of any kind.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the extension after changes means you accept the updated policy.
